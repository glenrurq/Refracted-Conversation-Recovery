Sumary

A laser microphone is an advanced listening device that uses a laser beam to detect vibrations in surfaces, such as windows or walls, and convert them into audio signals. This technology is used in security, investigation, and espionage applications, allowing conversations to be picked up remotely without physical contact with the sound source. It works by using reflected laser interference, which varies according to the sound vibrations on the target surface, resulting in a clear and precise audio signal.
